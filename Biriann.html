Birian

To cook biriani follow these steps:

1.Marinate Chicken/Meat: Mix meat with yogurt, spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala), ginger-garlic paste, and let it sit for 1-2 hours.

2.Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon).

3.Prepare Gravy: Saut√© onions, add marinated meat, tomatoes, and cook until tender.

4.Layering: In a pot, layer rice and gravy, top with fried onions, saffron milk, and ghee.

5.Dum Cooking: Cover and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes.

6.Serve: Mix gently and serve hot with raita.
